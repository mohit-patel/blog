Black Buck National Park

Oct 28, 2022 · Mohit Patel

I visited the Black Buck National Park in Velvadar (Bhavnagar) on 28th October 2022. This was my first trip to this park. It is protected area names after Black Bucks.

Landscape

The landscape is a gorgeous swathe of copper-gold grasses.

Black Buck

Males are black coloured with spiraling horns.

Blue Bull

Blue Bull, also known as Nilgai, is the Largest antelope of India.

Montagu Harrier

The park is home to many species of Harriers.

Eurasian Roller

This was my first sighting of Eurasian Roller; quite similar to Indian Roller, which I have seen in jungles of central India.
